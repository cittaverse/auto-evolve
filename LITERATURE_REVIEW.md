# CittaVerse Literature Review & Strategic Positioning (Updated: 2026-03-05)

## Theme 1: AI-Assisted Reminiscence Therapy (回忆疗法)
The field is currently experiencing a surge in LLM applications for conversational reminiscence therapy.
- **[2025] AI CHATBOT BUILT ON LARGE LANGUAGE MODELS FOR DELIVERY OF REMINISCENCE THERAPY TO SOCIALLY ISOLATED OLDER ADULTS: MEET Remi** (ScienceDirect)
  - *Key Takeaway*: Direct proof that LLM-based conversational bots for RT are entering clinical/social validation phases right now.
- **[2024] Retromind and the image of memories: a preliminary study of a support tool for reminiscence therapy** (Springer)
- **[2024] An Exploration of LLM-Guided Conversation in Reminiscence Therapy** (OpenReview)
  - *Key Takeaway*: Focuses on empathetic engagement in LLM-guided reminiscence.

## Theme 2: Computational Evaluation of Narrative Quality (计算化评估)
The transition from traditional NLP to LLMs is happening *this year*.
- **[2025] Modeling memories, predicting prospections: Automated scoring of autobiographical detail narration using large language models**
  - *Key Takeaway*: Fine-tuned LLaMA-3 (8B) as a continuous regression model to score internal/external details. Reached r=0.87 correlation with human raters.
- **[2024] Automated scoring of the autobiographical interview with natural language processing** (van Genugten & Schacter)
  - *Key Takeaway*: The immediate predecessor, attempting automated scoring before the LLM paradigm shift.

## Theme 3: Neuro-symbolic AI in Narrative/Memory (神经符号网络技术占位)
- **Market Gap**: Searches for "Neuro-symbolic" combined with "Autobiographical Interview scoring" or "Clinical narrative evaluation" yield **zero exact hits**. 
- **Strategic Value**: Pure LLMs (like the 2025 LLaMA-3 paper) act as black-box regression models. Clinical applications require explainability. A neuro-symbolic approach (LLM for semantic extraction + Symbolic rules for Levine's 2002 scoring manual) is a massive blue ocean for patents and high-impact papers.
