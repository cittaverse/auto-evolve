Dear arXiv Editors,

We submit our technical report on the CittaVerse Narrative Scorer v0.7.0, an automated assessment tool for Chinese autobiographical memory narratives in reminiscence therapy.

**Why cs.HC**: This work presents a user-centered system for digital mental health intervention, focusing on interpretable scoring and immediate clinical feedback.

**Key contributions**:
1. First automated narrative quality scorer for Chinese autobiographical memories
2. Six-dimension assessment aligned with reminiscence therapy research
3. Neuro-symbolic architecture (rule-based + LLM enhancement)
4. Deployed in ongoing pilot RCT (N=50, 2-week intervention)
5. Open-source (MIT license) with 85+ tests and full CI

**Status**: This is a technical report (not peer-reviewed journal submission).
